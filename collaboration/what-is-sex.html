  • What is sex? / I. and II. Itineration
    - project by Philipp Joy Reinhardt
Collaboration with Philipp Joy Reinhardt on “The Beekeepers” (diptych), 2025, alongside a contribution of embellished garments “Midnight Bliss Collection” to the group project “What Is Sex?”

The project, initiated by Philipp J. Reinhardt and developed in collaboration with multiple artists, was first presented during the final exhibition at @aka_akastudios in December 2025 and later in January 2026 at @kaputte_szene.

What Is Sex? — “A collective painting composed of garments created by participating artists. It unfolds through activation by visitors, functioning like a skin for social interaction to be worn.”
— Philipp J. Reinhardt
